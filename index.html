About

Hi! I'm Andres Arruti, a multidisciplinary Graphic Designer specialized in branding, packaging design and editorial design. At the end of 2021 I started my formation as a UX/UI Designer since it is what I want to dedicate myself to from now on.
I was born on October 25, 1990 in Argentina and have been working in the field of graphic communication since 2010.
During my last years at university and after obtaining my degree in Graphic and Advertising Design I have worked in a marketing agency as a Jr and Senior Graphic Designer, and I also work with my own freelance accounts.
I was part of a small but hard working company dedicated to printing, and participated in design projects as a collaborator with colleagues from my dear Argentina.
After that I moved to Italy and I get my italian citizenship, there I worked in a cosmetic lab and factory doing branding, packaging design and web pieces.
Later I decided to change course a bit and I started studying UX/UI Design, I was working on some own and collaborative product design projects.

Work Experience

Cosmomack Cosmetics laboratory. Rimini, Italy. www.cosmomack.com
I worked in the e-marketing team creating posts for social media and other graphic pieces for the web of the cosmetic lines that we commercialized. I designed brands, inner packagings (bottles) and outer packagings (boxes) for entire lines of own cosmetics and for clients from different countries. I made boxes of different sizes and special characteristics, dealing directly with our suppliers.

Agencia Zher Marketing Agency. Córdoba, Argentina. www.agenciazher.com
I led a team of 4 graphic designers deciding the creative ways for advertising graphic pieces, becoming responsable of the final artworks (flat graphics and videos). Weekly we worked on the design of an 8-pages product catalog, published digitally and printed in 600,000 weekly copies, POP pieces, radio and television spots. I designed marketing-stands and exhibitors for national and international brands, also I worked in the design and production of graphic pieces for set up supermarkets and commercial premises.

I worked in teams of 2 or 3 designers making graphic pieces including entire brands, web posts, packagings and advertising catalogs. I managed the final artworks for printing press. I did Illustrations for motion graphic content.

Mandala Print Printing press. Córdoba, Argentina.
I operated laser printers, guillotines, and other post-press machines and was in charge of managing our social media profiles.
